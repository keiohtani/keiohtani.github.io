About Kei Ohtani

His Career As A Photographer

Kei Ohtani is a portraits and events photographer located in Greencastle, Indiana. He is a student photographer at Communication Department at DePauw University as a member of Information Technology Associates Program, while he is pursuing a Bachelor Degree in Computer Science. His works were exhibited at Student Juried Show at DePauw University, and one of them was selected for the first place in 4D arts.
